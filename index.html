Every dish is a meditation on heat, time, and restraint — a philosophy we've honed across 17 years of pushing boundaries in one of the world's most vibrant dining scenes.

The Chef

A lifetime devoted
to the flame

Karim El-Masry trained under some of Europe's most demanding kitchens — from Lyon to Copenhagen — before returning to Cairo with a singular vision: to channel the primal power of fire into an entirely new Egyptian culinary language.

His approach merges classical French technique with deeply local ingredients and Middle Eastern spice philosophy, creating dishes that feel both ancient and radically modern.
